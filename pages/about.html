Since 2011

We started Marquee to fix the handover problem

After years inside venues and agencies, our founders kept seeing the same failure: brilliant suppliers, no one connecting them. Marquee was built to be the connective tissue, a studio that owns the whole event, not a slice of it.

Today we are a tight team of planners, designers and producers who treat your celebration with the seriousness it deserves and the lightness it should feel like.
