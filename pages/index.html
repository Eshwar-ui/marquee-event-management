How we work

A calm, four-step path to a flawless day

Every Marquee event follows the same considered rhythm, so nothing is left to chance and you always know exactly where things stand.

01

Consult

We listen first, mapping your vision, guest count and budget into a clear brief and concept direction.

02

Design

Moodboards, layouts and a curated vendor shortlist, all reviewable and approvable from your dashboard.

03

Coordinate

Timelines, guest lists and budgets stay in sync while we manage every supplier on your behalf.

04

Celebrate

On the day, our team runs the show end to end, so you are fully present for every moment.

Why Marquee

One studio, every detail, zero stress

Most planning falls apart in the gaps between vendors. Marquee closes them. We are a single point of accountability from concept to clean-up, with a client dashboard that keeps the whole plan transparent.

  • Live planning progress. Milestones and tasks update in real time, no chasing email threads.
  • Approve vendors in a click. Review quotes and portfolios, then approve or decline from one place.
  • Budgets that stay honest. Every line tracked against your cap, with no surprise on the final invoice.
